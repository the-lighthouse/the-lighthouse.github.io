Welcome

The Lighthouse is a non-denominational church in Sault Ste Marie, Ontario.

You are welcome to join our Sunday morning service at 10:30 am.

We currently meet at 302 Queen Street East at the corner of Bruce Street at the Summit Church building.

The Lighthouse Church

The Lighthouse exists to invite First Nations people and their friends into a relationship with our Creator God through Jesus. We seek to become fully devoted followers of Jesus Christ, living by His example and encouraging each other through our fellowship.

And Jesus came and said to them, “All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you. And behold, I am with you always, to the end of the age.” - Matthew 28:18-20.
