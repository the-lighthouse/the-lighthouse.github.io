The Lighthouse

A Church Welcoming First Nations People and Friends

The Lighthouse is a non-denominational church in Sault Ste Marie, Ontario.
You are welcome to join our Sunday morning service at 10:30 am.
We meet at 13 Salisbury Avenue, Sault Ste. Marie, ON P6B 1L8

About Us

The Lighthouse exists to invite First Nations people and their friends into a relationship with our Creator God through Jesus. We seek to become fully devoted followers of Jesus Christ, living by His example and encouraging each other through our fellowship.
